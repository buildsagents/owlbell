## WHY THIS WORKS

1. **Name rotation kills the spam feeling** — 15 different senders feels like a real company with real people, not one guy's Mailchimp account
2. **Loss aversion beats gain** — "you're losing $47k" beats "you could earn $47k" by 3x
3. **Concrete beats abstract** — "$297 flat" beats "affordable pricing" every time
4. **Trade-specific beats generic** — "summer heat + busy signals" for HVAC hits harder than "missed calls" for everyone
5. **Zero friction beats high-touch** — no "book a call" means no barrier to entry
6. **Short beats long** — 4-5 sentences read on a phone between jobsites
7. **Peer proof beats authority** — "300+ contractors" beats "industry-leading AI solution"
8. **Guarantee kills risk objection** — "free month if we don't book 5 jobs" is the closest thing to free the contractor will ever see
