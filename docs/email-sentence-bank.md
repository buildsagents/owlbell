# Email Sentence Bank — Plug & Play

Paste these into your templates. Each sentence is built to make a contractor stop scrolling and feel the pain.

---

## Pain Hooks (sentences 1-2)

- Every time your phone rings and you can't pick up, that job is already calling your next competitor.
- You're paying for leads. Voicemail is where those leads go to die.
- Your customers don't care you're busy — they just call the next guy.
- The jobs you don't answer are the ones your competitor is pricing right now.
- You can't answer while you're on a ladder. Voicemail doesn't book appointments.
- Every voicemail you send is a gift to your competitor's answering service.
- When you're on a roof and your phone rings, you're choosing between finishing the job and losing a lead. You shouldn't have to choose.
- Eight out of ten voicemails never get a callback. That's eight jobs gone before you even knew they called.
- Your phone doesn't stop ringing when you clock out — and neither do your competitors.

## The "I've Been There" Credibility

- I've watched good contractors lose thousands because they couldn't be two places at once.
- I've been on the tools. I know you don't have time to learn another piece of software.
- I ran a crew for 12 years. Missed calls kept me up at night.
- I've been in your boots. I know the feeling of checking voicemail at the end of the day and knowing those jobs are gone.
- I'm not a tech guy trying to sell you software. I'm someone who figured out how to solve a problem I used to have myself.
- I've seen too many good tradesmen go out of business not because their work was bad, but because they couldn't answer the phone.

## The Math That Hurts

- 10 missed calls a week × $250 average job × 50 weeks = $125,000 a year you're leaving on the table.
- One emergency call after hours pays for Owlbell for an entire year.
- You only need 2 extra jobs a year to cover the cost. Everything after that is pure profit.
- $297/mo. One job pays for it. Two jobs pays for it twice. You will never miss a call again for the price of half a job.
- If you miss 5 calls a week at $200 a job, that's $52,000 a year in calls that went to the guy who answered.
- Your voicemail is costing you more than your truck payment, your insurance, and your fuel combined.

## Zero-Friction Closer

- You don't install anything. You don't configure anything. You don't talk to anyone. Forward your number. We handle the rest.
- Our average setup takes 4 minutes and I don't even have to call you.
- No dashboard to learn. No passwords to remember. Just calls that get answered and jobs that get booked.
- We do literally everything. You forward one number and go back to work.
- No meetings. No onboarding calls. No training. Pick a plan, forward your number, done.
- If you can forward a phone call, you can use Owlbell. That's the only step.

## The "Your Competitor" Needle-Mover

- While you're reading this, there's a roofing company 3 miles from you that answers every single call at 11pm on a Sunday.
- Your competitor already has this. They're booking jobs while you're reading this email.
- The contractors using Owlbell aren't telling their competitors. You found us first.
- There's a plumber in your area answering 2am flood calls right now while everyone else's voicemail picks up.
- Every day you wait is another day your competitor answers calls that should've been yours.

- Try it for a month. If we don't book you 5 jobs, you don't pay. That's not a gimmick, that's how sure we are.
